Data File: ADDHW1N(Description Only Codebook)

1) AID
Respondent Identifier


2) SIZE
Number of questionnaires in school


3) IDGX2
In-degree: The number of times ego is nominated by other students in the school


4) ODGX2
Out-degree: The number of people respondent nominates in the school


5) NOUTNOM
Number of nominations outside the school: The number of people ego nominates from outside the school


6) TAB113
Number of matched nominations to sister school: The number of valid alters ego nominates from the sister school


7) BCENT10X
Bonacich centrality, B=0.1: Ego's centrality, weighted by the centrality of those to whom he/she sends ties


8) REACH
Reach: Maximum number of alters ego can reach in the total friendship network


9) REACH3
Reach in three steps: A step refers to the length of a path from i to j. If i nominates j and j nominates k and k nominates l, then i and l are three steps apart. The value of the variable REACH3 is the total number of alters ego can reach in three steps.


10) IGDMEAN
Mean geodesic distance: Average path length between ego and ego's complete set of reachable alters.


11) PRXPREST
Proximity prestige: Measures the prestige of ego relative to the number of people who can reach ego


12) INFLDMN
Influence domain: Number of alters who can reach ego


13) HAVEBMF
Ego has a best male friend: Dummy variable indicating whether ego nominated a male friend in the school as his/her best friend


14) HAVEBFF
Ego has a best female friend: Dummy variable indicating whether ego nominated a female friend in the school as his/her best friend


15) BMFRECIP
Best male friend reciprocates: Dummy variable indicating whether the person ego nominated as his/her best male friend nominated ego a friend


16) BMFRECBF
Best male friend reciprocates as best friend: Dummy variable indicating whether the person ego nominated as his/her best male friend nominated ego as his best friend


17) BFFRECIP
Best female friend reciprocates: Dummy variable indicating whether the person ego nominated as his/her best female friend nominated ego as any type of friend


18) BFFRECBF
Best female friend reciprocates as best friend: Dummy variable indicating whether the person ego nominated as his/her best female friend nominated ego as her best friend


19) ESDEN
Ego send-network density: Density of the network composed of ego and the set of alters nominated by ego


20) NES
Size of ego send-network: Number of alters nominated by ego, plus ego


21) ERDEN
Ego receive-network density: Density of the network composed of ego and the set of alters who nominate ego


22) NER
Size of ego receive-network: Number of alters who nominate ego, plus ego


23) ESRDEN
Ego send- and receive-network density: Density of the network composed of ego, the set of alters nominated by ego, and the set of alters who nominate ego


24) NESR
Size of ego send- and receive-network: Number of alters who are nominated by ego or who nominate ego, plus ego


25) EHSGRD
Ego send-network heterogeneity: grade (heterogeneity score)


26) ERSNGRD

27) NEHSGRD

32) ERNGRD

33) NEHGRD
